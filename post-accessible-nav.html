Accessible Mobile Navigation in ~60 lines

  • 02 Oct 2025
  • • —
  • accessibilityuijavascript

This pattern handles keyboard trap, ESC/outside click to close, and ARIA wiring without libraries.

  1. Attach aria-controls, track aria-expanded.
  2. Trap focus inside the menu when open; restore to invoker on close.
  3. Close on Esc, outside click, or link activation.
