An introduction to myself

Leanne Douglass

Hello! I'm Leanne Douglass, a Computer Science student at Aston University with a strong passion for technology, creativity, and problem-solving. My academic journey has given me a solid foundation in programming, data structures, and AI — skills I'm now using to build real-world digital solutions.


Before entering the tech world, I developed my leadership and people-management skills through a career in fashion retail and the health & beauty industry, where I currently work in a senior management role. These experiences taught me the importance of clear communication, customer experience, and leading high‑performing teams.


I'm now pursuing a future in freelance web development and AI development, with a focus on creating innovative, accessible, and user-friendly digital products. I love combining technical precision with design thinking to bring ideas to life online.
